YOUR FOOTBALL CAREER

IS OUR GOAL

MySportale is an online scouting network and a social platform for all football players, coaches, scouts and fans.

Our networking platfrom is targeted at young players from the age of 6-16 who are looking to develop a football career. We give you the opportunity to tell us your football story and help you spread the word to the world

Everyone can create a FREE account and communicate with others to grow their network. Users can create football events and tag their friends, teamates etc. Players can recieve a coach report by one of our reputable coaches and get ranked by guests on free style ("virtuoso") videos.
